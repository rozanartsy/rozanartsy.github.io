Hi, my name is Kateryna Struhalova!
My artist name is Rozan.

I’m from Ukraine and live in a city by the sea.

I graduated from university in 2019 in Warsaw, Poland, where I studied Graphic Arts.
During my second year, I realized that creating and imagining things for children was what truly resonated with me.
That’s when my journey into children’s illustration began.

I aim to create warm, whimsical worlds that spark curiosity and joy.

I enjoy exploring different styles, and I love playing with unique color palettes and rich textures.
